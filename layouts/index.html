Speakers' Spotlight

Here's a sneak peak of what you can expect this year!

Scott Amyx

CEO    Amyx+McKinsey
Scot Amyx is the CEO of Amyx+McKinsey. He is a thought leader in wearables and the Internet of Things, and industry member of AllSeen Alliance, writes for Wired, InformationWeek, IEEE, Wearable Technologies, and speaks at global conferences. Previously, as VP of Product Management, Scott helped the company be acquired by a Fortune 500 publicly traded company. Scott has a MPP from the University of Chicago and is a national Woodrow Wilson / Sloan Fellow.

John Vaskis

Senior Director of Hardware Outreach and Head of Partnerships at Indiegogo
John Vaskis is the Senior Director of Hardware at Indiegogo where he works closely with hardware to build go to market strategies for their product launches and beyond. He has worked over a dozen million dollar campaigns across all vertical..

Previously John worked at Oberon Media a white label digital distribution channel that included AOL, MSN Yahoo and Orange. John headed up a team that managed relationships for over 350 game developers including Electronic Arts, Activision and TellTale Games.
